Carbonara

Description

How to make carbonara.

  • Pasta
  • Three eggs
  • All sorts of bacon.
  • Parmigiano
  1. Cook the noodles in salty water.
  2. Fry onoins, garlic, thyme, rosmarin, all sorts of bacon.
  3. Put the pasta into the pan, add three mashed eggs and a lot of parmesan, while putting out the stove.
  4. Mix it and serve it.
